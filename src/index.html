Heaton Dainard acquisitions in the Seattle area

Heaton Dainard advertises its ability to make smart investments during market volatility. The firm has purchased or represented investors to purchase 570 homes over the past 15 years, including 240 during the real estate market collapse of 2008-2014. The acquisitions concentrate in gentrifying neighborhoods like the Central District, Rainier Valley, Lake City and West Seattle.
